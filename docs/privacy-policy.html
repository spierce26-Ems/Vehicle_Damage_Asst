Privacy Policy

Vehicle Damage Investigation Assistant — Last updated: July 17, 2026

Vehicle Damage Investigation Assistant ("the App," "we," "our") is built around a simple principle: your case data stays on your device. This policy explains exactly what information the App accesses, why, and — just as importantly — what we do not collect, transmit, or store anywhere else.

1. Summary

  • The App has no backend server and does not transmit your case data, photos, or location information anywhere.
  • All photos, measurements, notes, and generated reports are stored only in the App's private storage on your device (and in Apple's iCloud backup of your device, if you have iCloud device backup turned on — the App itself does not sync to any cloud service).
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs of any kind.
  • We do not collect, sell, or share any personal data with third parties.
  • Purchases (one-time case unlocks and Pro subscriptions) are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit — we never see or store your payment information.

2. Information the App Accesses On Your Device

The App requests the following device permissions, used only for the purposes described:

PermissionPurpose
Camera To capture photos of vehicle damage directly within the App for your case documentation.
Photo Library (read) To let you import existing damage photos already on your device into a case, instead of re-taking them.
Photo Library (add) To let you save a generated report or photo back to your Photos app, if you choose to.
Location (While Using the App) To record where a damage photo was captured, so your case documentation includes the incident location. This is stored only inside that case's data on your device.

You can review or revoke any of these permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security on your device. The App will continue to work for case management and reporting even if a permission is declined, with the corresponding feature simply unavailable.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Case records — including photos, vehicle details, damage measurements, notes, and generated PDF reports — are stored in the App's own private, sandboxed storage area on your device (Documents/Cases), using standard iOS file storage. This data:

  • Is never uploaded to any server operated by us.
  • Is included in your device's normal iCloud/iTunes backup only if you have that turned on at the iOS level — this is standard Apple device-backup behavior for any app, not something the App does on its own.
  • Is deleted when you delete a case within the App, or when you delete the App itself.

4. On-Device Technical Data

To operate correctly, the App reads limited technical metadata that never leaves your device:

  • File timestamps — when listing your saved cases, the App reads the last-modified date of files inside its own private storage folder, purely to sort and display your case list. This never accesses files outside the App's own container and is never transmitted anywhere.
  • App preferences (UserDefaults) — the App stores a small amount of local preference data on your device, such as your remaining one-time case-unlock credit balance. This is plain on-device app storage, not shared with any other app, service, or App Group, and is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Purchases

One-time case unlocks and Pro subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We receive only the confirmation that a purchase succeeded so the App can unlock the relevant feature — we never receive or store your name, card number, or other payment details. Apple's own privacy practices govern that transaction; see Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Analytics, Advertising & Tracking

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites, and the App does not use Apple's Advertising Identifier (IDFA).

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, as the App collects no information off-device in the first place.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date at the top of the page.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or the App's data practices can be directed to the developer via the contact information listed on the App's App Store page.
